Routines

Biceps

Biceps Biceps Biceps! Everyone wants those big arms to wear a nice sleeveless shirt during the summer, here we can teach you some routines to get some nice toned arms
Below is a basic list of easy bicep exercise to get you that nice baseball shaped biceps

  • 3 sets of 10x biceps curls (free weights)
  • 3 sets of 10x cable curls
  • 3 sets of 10x push ups (close grip)
  • 3 sets of 10x barbell reverse grip

Triceps

Alot of people don't realize that triceps are actually 2/3 of your entire arm, so the secret to big arms...thats right, Triceps!
Below are some killer triceps exercises, making sure to give you a perfect pump

  • 2 sets of 10x tricep presses
  • 3 sets of diamond pushups
  • 3 sets of triceps push downs with cables

Upper Body

This encompasses back and chest. Some of the biggest muscles in your body. With a strong back and chest, your posture automatically comes into play, and even if you dont loose weight, you look alot more toned.
Below are some basic upper body chest and back exercises

  • 3 sets of 5x deadlifts
  • 2 sets of 10x lat pull downs
  • Wide grip pull up

  • Chest press barebell
  • Dumbell press 10 reps of 10
  • Cables chest press

Lower Body

One of the most neglected parts of the body people miss are the legs. You do not want to go around with big arms, big chest, and big everything, but small legs. You will look like a chicken.
Below is a list of exercises that will help you get the legs you deserve

'
  • 3 sets of 5x leg presses
  • 10 sets of low weight squats
  • 3 sets of 5x machine curls
  • 3 sets of 5x weighted squats
